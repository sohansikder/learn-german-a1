Culture & Etiquette

Language isn't just words; it's understanding the people.

🤝

Du vs. Sie

Sie is formal — used with strangers, elders, and in business. Du is informal — for friends, family, and children.

⏱️

Pünktlichkeit

Punctuality is highly valued in Germany. If a meeting is at 14:00, arriving at 13:55 is "on time". Being 5 minutes late requires an apology.

🌍

Mülltrennung

Germans take recycling seriously. You must separate your trash into: Papier (Paper), Plastik/Verpackung (Plastic), Restmüll (General), and Biomüll (Organic).
